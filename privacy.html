ZapCaptcha Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025
Last Updated: July 4, 2025

1. Introduction

ZapCaptcha is a client-side, privacy-preserving CAPTCHA solution developed by QVLx Labs. This Privacy Policy outlines what data (if any) is collected when users interact with ZapCaptcha-protected forms or actions.

2. No Data Collection

ZapCaptcha does not collect or transmit any personal information, identifiers, cookies, IP addresses, or behavioral analytics. The CAPTCHA runs entirely in the browser and does not rely on third-party tracking or server-side calls.

3. Local Storage & Cookies

ZapCaptcha may use short-lived browser cookies or sessionStorage/localStorage to store a cryptographic nonce for session replay protection. These values are never transmitted and are cleared automatically by browser policy.

4. No Third-Party Scripts

ZapCaptcha does not embed or load any third-party content, fonts, or analytics scripts. All logic is self-contained and served from trusted QVLx Labs infrastructure or your own static hosting.

5. Accessibility & Consent

ZapCaptcha is designed to be unobtrusive and accessible. It does not require explicit user consent under GDPR/CCPA as no personally identifiable information is collected or processed.

6. Use in Your Application

If you are using ZapCaptcha in your own product or website, it is your responsibility to ensure your own privacy practices comply with relevant laws. ZapCaptcha itself imposes no user tracking or data sharing burden.

7. Questions

For inquiries about ZapCaptcha privacy or its implementation, contact QVLx Labs at security@qvlx.com.
